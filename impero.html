Unified control in a single interface

Opses IMPERO is a complete control system that interfaces the control of external equipment to the vehicle’s touch screen, negating unsightly hardware control panels and buttons that would otherwise require fitting on the dash.

learn more

Why IMPERO?

At its core, the IMPERO platform connects directly into the OEM touchscreen through our VERTO switch allowing the driver to seamlessly change between the existing infotainment system and the IMPERO software platform, using a configurable gesture-based swipe.

The IMPERO system significantly reduces the amount of dashboard alterations required improving driver safety and vehicle resale value.

Traditional control panels and physical buttons are replaced with virtual buttons, swipes and taps. Fingertip control is provided centrally and ergonomically on the vehicle’s inbuilt OEM touchscreen. Radio systems, emergency warning systems, ANPR camera systems and in-vehicle data systems are safely accessed from the factory fitted touch screen.

Key Features

01

Navigation Control With Live Traffic

IMPERO is fitted with a full navigation solution and can be configured for control rooms to instigate navigation to an incident

02

Emergency Services Radio Control

Industry standard radio systems are controlled from the touch screen using a simpler and safer user interface. Special functionality can be added such as control room instigated talkgroup/channel changes

03

Full Emergency Warning Equipment Control

All emergency light functions are available through a customisable user interface with full macro functionality. Direct interface to industry standard control systems such as the MCS series of controllers.

04

In-Vehicle Data Terminals

Full integration of Windows and Android data terminals through screen replication and touch screen control.

05

Gesture-based Control

Large, clear fonts with striking contrast are easier to read in stressful situations. The most important functions of the radio can be controlled by natural gestures with dedicated positions on the screen.
